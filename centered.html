Anti-Doomscrolling App

Your attention is the most precious thing you own.

Centered helps you reclaim it — not through willpower or rigid blocking, but through awareness, intention, and sustainable habits.

By the time you've opened Instagram, your willpower is already at its lowest. That's by design. Centered intervenes before the loop begins.

Three Layers of Support

Designed to meet you at every stage of the habit cycle

01
Before Opening

Prevention

Intercept the impulse before the engagement loop starts — the only moment you're still in control.

  • Surface your morning intention
  • Show your real-time open count
  • Offer one-tap better alternatives
02
During Use

Harm Reduction

When you do scroll, gentle nudges keep you conscious — without nagging or shame.

  • Smart timer awareness prompts
  • Loop detection (same content twice)
  • "What are you looking for?" check-ins
03
Long-term

Habit Building

The goal is independence — building self-awareness until you no longer need the app.

  • Track what you did instead
  • Positive streaks, not shame streaks
  • Weekly reflections & pattern insights

Our Philosophy

"The goal is not abstinence — it's intentionality. You decide when and why you scroll."

Social media has legitimate uses. Centered doesn't punish connection — it restores your agency over when you choose it. Conscious choices replace automatic habits, one moment at a time.
